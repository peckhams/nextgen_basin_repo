Note: To show vertices of a polygon in QGIS.
1. In the Layers box, right click on the layer.
2. Choose Styles > Edit Symbol...
3. Click plus sign (upper right) to "add symbol layer"
4. In "Symbol layer type" droplist, choose: "Outline: Marker Line"
5. In "Marker placement" section, check "On first vertex" and
   "On inner vertices".
6. Click on OK button.
